GrizzHacks: The Beach Episode

GrizzHacks is a 24 hour engineering event that is welcome to all students no matter the level of developer you are. During the 24 hours of the event each team will make a brand new project then present that project to judges and compete for prizes. You are allowed to create anything you would like, but we have specific tracks that you could follow if you can't think of a project. There will be multiple tracks and challenges for you to participate in to win prizes.
